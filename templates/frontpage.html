Welcome to the AWI AI COATING TOOL

The AWI AI COATING TOOL is a powerful tool designed using AI and optical concepts to help engineers and researchers model and simulate optical coatings. It enables you to design complex multilayer structures and evaluate their optical properties, such as reflectance, transmittance, and absorptance, across a range of wavelengths.

Whether you're working with Bragg reflectors, antireflective coatings, or any other optical stack, this simulator provides accurate results based on input parameters like layer thickness, material properties, and incoherence levels. It is especially useful for optimizing your design and improving the performance of optical systems.

You can customize the stack's design, simulate light propagation, and optimize the thicknesses of the layers to achieve the desired optical performance. It's an essential tool for anyone involved in optical engineering, material science, or photonics research.
